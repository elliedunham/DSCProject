Field Hockey

Field hockey is a sport played around the world. With 10 field players and a goalie, the game is set on a 100 yard astro-turf pitch. The field is lined with two 25-yard lines. In order to score, the ball must be shot within the shooting circle. The shooting circle is improperly named as it is actually shaped as a semi-circle.

An important rule of the game is the ball cannot touch the feet of any player, except the goalies, who are dressed in thick pads.
